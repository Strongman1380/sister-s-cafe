About Sister’s Café

Tucked in the heart of Geneva, Nebraska, Sister’s Café is more than just a place to eat—it’s a place to feel at home. Founded by Adina Eide and her family, our café carries on a proud tradition of made-from-scratch comfort food, heartfelt service, and small-town hospitality that turns every meal into a memory. As a family-owned and operated diner, we believe food is love—and you can taste that in every bite. From the first sizzle on the griddle in the morning to the last plate served at lunch, everything we make is rooted in care, tradition, and flavor. We proudly serve classic American breakfasts, hearty lunch plates, and rotating daily specials that keep our regulars coming back again and again. Craving a fluffy stack of pancakes, a crispy breakfast burrito, or our famous hot beef sandwich with mashed potatoes and gravy? We’ve got you covered. Need a sweet finish? Don’t leave without trying one of Adina’s fresh-baked cinnamon rolls—they’ve become a local legend. Whether you’re a longtime neighbor or just passing through town, you’ll be greeted with a smile, a warm cup of coffee, and a seat at the table. At Sister’s Café, we’re here to serve more than meals—we’re here to serve community, comfort, and connection.
